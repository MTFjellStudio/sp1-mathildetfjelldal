Exhibition Spaces

We believe science should not be confined to the textbook, but brought to life through exhibits. Discover over 1000 different interactive exhibits across our varied subjects.

  • Cosmology

    Explore the wonders of our cosmos. Our fantastic exhibition, 'The Sky Above Us', explores the night sky and what we can see and know about the universe around us.

  • Evolution

    Travel back in time and meet our resident Woolly Mammoth. Discover how life has adapted and changed over millions of years of Earth's history.

  • AI & Robotics

    Step into the information revolution! Discover how robotics and artificial intelligence are shaping the future, and even shake hands with Rob the Robot.

  • Biology & Medicine

    From the smallest cells to the largest mammals, explore the very building blocks of life itself in our highly interactive biology wing.

  • Ecology

    Explore how to create a sustainable future for the whole planet. Unpack the dangers of global warming while discovering positive ways we can all contribute.

  • Temporary Exhibits

    Energetica

    Experience the power of the elements! On loan from Amsterdam, this special installation lets you harness solar energy and wind turbines to create power.
