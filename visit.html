Plan Your Visit

We are excited to welcome you to the Community Science Museum. Whether you are exploring the cosmos or traveling back in time, here is everything you need to know to plan a comfortable and inspiring day with us.

Getting Here

Public Transit

The museum is easily accessible by public transportation. Bus lines 12, 45, and 72 stop directly outside the main entrance at the "Science Center" stop.

The central train station is just a short 10-minute walk away.

Parking

If you are arriving by car, a paid underground parking garage is located beneath the museum, offering direct elevator access to the lobby.

Limited metered street parking is also available in the surrounding neighborhood.

Accessibility

The museum has been architecturally designed to ensure universal access and a seamless experience for all visitors.

Our entire building is fully wheelchair accessible, with wide, spacious corridors and ramps.

Elevators are conveniently located on every floor.

Sensory-friendly hours, featuring reduced noise and dimmed lighting, are available every Tuesday morning from 10:00 to 12:00.

Admission

We believe science should be accessible to everyone, which is why general admission to our permanent exhibits is completely free!

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.

If you would like to organize a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

Facilities

Free coat rooms and secure lockers are available near the main entrance to store your bags and coats.

Fully accessible restrooms, complete with family-friendly changing facilities, are located on all levels of the museum.
