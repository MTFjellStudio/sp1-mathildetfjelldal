Cosmology

Explore the wonders of our cosmos. Our fantastic exhibition, ‘The Sky Above Us’, explores the night sky and what we can see and know about the universe around us.

Exhibition Highlights

The Night Sky

For millennia, humans have looked upwards and wondered.

Our fantastic exhibition, 'The Sky Above Us', explores the night sky and everything we can observe from our own backyard.

Step into our immersive stargazing room, locate various constellations, discover how galaxies are formed, and learn how early navigators used the North Star and the Southern Cross as a compass to guide their ships across vast, uncharted oceans.

Solar Exploration

The quest to understand our place in the universe is a story of incredible human ingenuity.

Follow the journey of solar exploration through the ages. Learn how early astronomers mapped the stars with astrolabes, and see a replica of the ground-breaking telescope Galileo used to discover the moons of Jupiter.

From the earliest stargazers to modern day rovers currently sending photographs back from the surface of Mars, trace the technological leaps that let us reach the planets.

The Sky Above Us

What exactly are you looking at when you see a shooting star?

In this interactive exhibit, you can explore the origins of light and matter. Get hands-on with real meteorites that have crashed to Earth, discover the science behind solar eclipses, and learn how to identify the planets in our solar system that are visible to the naked eye.

The universe is vast, but your journey into it starts right here.
